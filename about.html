About Pathways Within Wellness

A sanctuary for relaxation, rejuvenation, and renewal on Long Island.

Our Story

Wellness Begins Here

Pathways Within Wellness was created to honor what we deeply believe: wellness is holistic, involving body, spirit, mind, and earth.

Founded by Rachel Lessard, LCSW, as an extension of our mental health practice, our wellness spa provides a space where you can find physical relaxation and rejuvenation that complements emotional and mental well-being.

We've assembled a team of skilled, licensed, and passionate practitioners who share our commitment to helping you feel your best—inside and out.

Our Philosophy

The four pillars of our approach.

Body

Physical wellness through massage, skin care, and therapeutic treatments that release tension and restore vitality.

Spirit

Nurturing the soul through energy work, Reiki, and practices that support spiritual alignment and inner peace.

Mind

Mental clarity through stress relief, relaxation, and treatments that calm the nervous system.

Earth

Environmental consciousness through organic products, sustainable practices, and respect for nature.

Our Commitment to You

Organic & Sustainable Products

We use environmentally friendly, organic products that are good for your body and the planet. You'll never find harsh chemicals or synthetic ingredients in our treatments.

Expert Practitioners

Every member of our team is skilled, licensed, and genuinely passionate about helping you feel better. We invest in ongoing training to bring you the latest in wellness innovation.

Personalized Care

No two people are alike. We take the time to understand your unique needs and customize every treatment to deliver the best possible results for you.

Tranquil Environment

Our spa is designed as a sanctuary—a place to escape the stresses of daily life and immerse yourself in calm. From the moment you walk in, you'll feel the difference.

Holistic Approach

We don't just treat symptoms. We address the whole person, integrating physical relaxation with mental calm and spiritual renewal.

Integration with Therapy
