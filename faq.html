Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our spa services.

Booking & Appointments

You can book online through our contact form, call us at 516-336-9209, or email admin@pathwayswithinwellness.com. We'll confirm your appointment within 24 hours.
We require 24-hour notice for cancellations. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a fee. We understand emergencies happen—please let us know as soon as possible.
Please arrive 10-15 minutes before your scheduled appointment. This allows time for paperwork (if it's your first visit), changing, and settling in before your treatment begins.

Services & Treatments

Wear comfortable clothing. For massage and body treatments, you'll change into a robe or be properly draped with sheets. Your comfort and privacy are always respected. We provide robes, slippers, and everything you need.
Yes, we offer pregnancy massage with practitioners trained in prenatal care. However, we typically recommend waiting until after the first trimester. Please inform us of your pregnancy when booking so we can ensure appropriate care and positioning.
Please let us know about any allergies or sensitivities when booking and remind your practitioner before your treatment. We use organic products and can accommodate most sensitivities. For severe allergies, we may recommend a patch test before certain treatments.
Not sure which service to choose? Contact us and we'll help you find the right treatment based on your goals. Whether you're seeking relaxation, pain relief, skin rejuvenation, or spiritual alignment, we can recommend the best option for you.

Payment & Insurance

We accept NYSHIP insurance for massage therapy. For other services, please inquire. We also offer flexible payment options, package discounts, and membership programs.
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, cash, and HSA/FSA cards. Payment is due at the time of service.
Yes! Gift cards are available in any denomination and can be used for any service. They're perfect for birthdays, holidays, or just because. Available in physical or digital format.
Gratuity is appreciated but never required. If you enjoyed your service, a tip of 15-20% is customary. You can tip in cash or add it to your card payment.
