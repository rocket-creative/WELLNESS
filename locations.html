What to Expect

Before Your Visit

  • Book your appointment online or by phone
  • Arrive 10-15 minutes early for paperwork
  • Wear comfortable clothing
  • Avoid heavy meals right before certain treatments
  • Let us know about any health concerns or allergies

During Your Visit

  • Our staff will greet you and show you to your treatment room
  • Your practitioner will discuss your goals and any concerns
  • Relax and enjoy your treatment
  • After your service, take your time before leaving

Amenities

  • Private treatment rooms
  • Organic, environmentally friendly products
  • Relaxation areas
  • Complimentary water and tea
  • Clean, calming atmosphere

Parking & Accessibility

Massapequa Location

Free parking is available in our lot directly in front of the building. Handicap accessible parking is available near the entrance.

Garden City Location

Street parking is available on Franklin Ave. Additional parking can be found in nearby municipal lots. Our lower level entrance is accessible via elevator.
